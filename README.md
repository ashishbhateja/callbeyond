# The Call Beyond — Digital Edition

The Call Beyond is the monthly magazine of Sri Aurobindo Ashram, Delhi — a publication dedicated to the integral philosophy of Sri Aurobindo and the Mother. This repository hosts the digital edition of the magazine, built as a lightweight, AI-enhanced platform for seekers of Indian spiritual philosophy.

## About The Call Beyond

The Call Beyond magazine carries forward the spiritual and philosophical teachings of Sri Aurobindo Ashram, making its timeless wisdom accessible to a digital audience. The magazine explores themes of:

- **Integral Yoga** — The path of spiritual transformation through Sri Aurobindo's teachings
- **Indian Philosophy** — Classical and modern interpretations of Vedanta, Tantra, and Synthesis
- **Contemporary Spirituality** — Applying ancient wisdom to modern life challenges
- **Cultural Reflections** — Art, literature, and thought aligned with Ashram values

## Project Goal

This open-source project aims to create an AI-powered personalized reading experience for the magazine, where readers can:

1. Discover articles tailored to their spiritual journey and interests
2. Access curated content based on themes and philosophical depth
3. Receive AI-generated summaries and guided reflections on selected readings

## Technology Stack

- Static HTML/CSS — Lightweight, fast-loading pages for universal accessibility
- GitHub Pages — Free hosting with automatic deployment
- Planned: AI integration layer for content personalization (Codex Security)
