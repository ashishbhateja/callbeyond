# Vision

*The Call Beyond* has carried the integral philosophy of Sri Aurobindo and the
Mother for decades. The aim of this digital edition is not only to **publish**
that work, but to help a new generation **engage** with it — actively, personally,
and through their own questions.

Reading is the starting point, not the end. The open framework in this repository
is the foundation for an engagement layer that turns time-tested philosophy and
Indian scriptures from something you *read* into something you can *work with*.

## Where this is going

- **Personal, not passive.** On-device recommendations already adapt to a reader's
  themes (built — see the README). Next: guided reflections and practice prompts
  that invite a reader to sit with an idea rather than scroll past it.
- **Ask the texts.** A grounded question-answering layer over the magazine's
  archive and the scriptures it draws on, so a seeker can ask a real question —
  *"what does integral yoga say about doubt?"* — and be led back into the source
  material, **with citations, never invented answers.**
- **Thematic journeys.** Curated, adaptive paths through the year and the archive
  for someone new to the philosophy. A first journey — the **2026 editorial arc**
  (three movements, twelve themed months, festival anchors, the January⇄December
  mirror) — is already in the reader; richer, personalized paths come next.
- **A wider, younger audience.** Short dynamic entry points, multiple languages,
  and accessible-by-default design — so the material reaches seekers who would
  never pick up a print magazine.

## Principles that don't change

Each of these is an AI-assisted feature built on open, inspectable code, with the
content kept gated and every answer **traceable to its source**. The commitments
stay constant: legible over clever, the reader in control of their own data, and
the philosophy — not the technology — at the centre.

This is the direction. What is built today (see the [README](README.md)) is the
honest foundation for it, not a claim that the destination has been reached.
